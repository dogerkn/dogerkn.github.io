# Patch notes for 2.10b - Crash fixes & holiday tweaks

A handful of bug fixes — mostly crashes that could pop up in specific situations, but more importantly a fix for the DIA mission getting hard-stuck at "Mission Passed".

Also a small correction to how long holidays will appear for:
+ Easter: **Mar 31–Apr 8**
+ Halloween: **Oct 24–31**
+ Christmas: **Dec 15–31**

This means Easter is already over! Yay or nay?

## What's new:

+ **Fixed crash in mission reward screen**
+ **Fixed several font-related crashes on load**
    - _A race condition could cause the canvas renderer to crash when custom fonts hadn't finished loading yet — patched across a few callsites_
+ **Holiday theme detection now uses specific date ranges**

## What's next

+ The PixiJS 4→8 engine upgrade is still ongoing in the background — it's coming along nicely, but lots of things are still slightly crooked - _lots of other things are getting enormous performance boosts and various optimizations!_
+ **Also cooking up something unexpected for Steam... stay tuned!**
